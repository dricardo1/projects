Does your company perform state emission inspections?
No.  Please contact your local DMV for locations


What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept Cash, all major credit and debit cards on vehicle site. Credit and debit card purchases are also available online

How far will your travel to perform my inspection?
Our normal distance is a 50 mile radius around major cities


Will your technician take pictures of the vehicle?
Yes.  All of our inspection packages include photos


When will I receive the inspection report and photos?
Depending on their work load, our Technicians do the best to
complete the reports within a few hours after the inspection


​Is there any guarantee to the Technicians report?
We only guarantee that all items on report have been checked
thoroughly.  Any concerns or discrepancies please contact us



Does your Technician check for body or frame damage?
They only perform a visual check and conclude with a educated determination, if the signs of body/frame repairs are apparent. A CARFAX report can also show a collision if it was reported to them.
